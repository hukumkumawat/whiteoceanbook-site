Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

WhiteOceanBook (“we”, “our”, “the app”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information we collect

The app does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not access your contacts, photos, location, device microphone, camera, or local files.

Data stored on your device

Your quiz progress, accuracy, bookmarks, and app settings (like dark mode) are saved only on your own device. This data never leaves your phone and is not sent to us or anyone else. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

3. Advertisements & Analytics

To keep our educational content free for students, the app may display advertisements using third-party services. These service providers may access limited device information (such as your device model, OS version, and mobile network) to serve relevant non-personalized or personalized ads.

Specifically, third-party SDKs like Google AdMob may access your device’s Google Advertising ID (GAID) to limit ad frequency or customize ad delivery. You can easily reset or opt-out of personalized tracking through your phone's Android settings under Settings -> Google -> Ads.

4. Third-Party Service Links

The app uses third-party services that have their own independent privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy

Children

The app is suitable for students and the general public and does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
